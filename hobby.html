Hobbies.

3D modelling (blender)

As i learn more about the world of 3D and 3D rendering i have been creating more and more 3D models. while things like three.js and even 3D in vanilla javascript have peaked my interest, my main interest in this hobby lies with blender. Blender is a free 3D engine that allows modelling, rigging and animation.

Guitar

I have an interest in almost any musical instrument though guitar has been my staple for many many years. When i was younger i even competed in a uk wide battle of the bands for colleges. My band at the time won in Ireland and Scotland. We did not place in London but the experience was fantastic.

Digital Art

Another hobby is digital painting. Originally I used photoshop for this but ever since I got a surface go I have been using Krita. this is a more artist centric software.

Games

Ever since i was young i have always enjoyed games. I currently enjoy games with high difficulty levels. learning the intricacies of some games is as deep as learning a musical instrument. I also love games with great story and design. possibly the continuous problem solving that is required in games may have even been a catalyst in my enjoyment of coding.
